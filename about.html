About Our Organization

Our organization is dedicated to fostering inclusive and equitable public spaces through innovative AI solutions. We collaborate with community groups, urban designers, and AI researchers to ensure that diverse needs are met in urban design processes.

Our Mission

To democratize urban space visualization tools, empowering marginalized communities to influence the design and development of the spaces they occupy.

Our Team

Our multidisciplinary team comprises experts in AI, urban planning, and community engagement, all working together to create tools that align with human values and preferences.
