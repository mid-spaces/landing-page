MID-Space Dataset

The MID-Space dataset is a comprehensive resource designed to align AI-generated visualizations with diverse community preferences in urban public space design. Below are the details and download links for the dataset.

Dataset Components

  • Prompts: 3,350 diverse textual prompts covering various public space scenarios.
  • Images: AI-generated images using Stable Diffusion XL for each prompt.
  • Annotations: 42,131 annotations based on six criteria: accessibility, safety, diversity, inclusivity, invitingness, and comfort.
