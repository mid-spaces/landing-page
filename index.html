About the MID-Space Dataset

The MID-Space dataset is designed to bridge the gap between AI-generated visualizations and diverse communities’ preferences in urban public space design. It includes textual prompts, AI-generated images, and annotations based on criteria such as accessibility, safety, diversity, inclusivity, invitingness, and comfort.

  • 3,350 diverse prompts covering various public space scenarios
  • 42,131 annotations from 16 diverse participants
  • Integrated with Stable Diffusion XL for enhanced alignment with human preferences
